Privacy Policy

Neonide  ·  Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Neonide ("we", "our", "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile game. By playing Neonide, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Neonide stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game progress (current level, highest level completed)
  • In-game currency (coins) balance
  • Purchased and selected cosmetic skins
  • Music preference (on/off)

We do not create user accounts, and we do not transmit personal information to our servers.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Neonide displays rewarded video advertisements provided by Google AdMob. When you watch an ad, AdMob may collect and process certain data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID / IDFA)
  • Approximate location (country/region)
  • App usage and interaction data

You can reset or opt out of personalised advertising at any time through your device settings (Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads; iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking).

3. In-App Purchases

If you make an in-app purchase, the transaction is processed entirely by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not receive or store your payment details. All purchases are subject to the respective store's terms and privacy policy.

4. Children's Privacy

Neonide is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

5. Third-Party Services

The following third-party SDKs may be present in the app:

  • Google AdMob – Rewarded video advertising
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Billing – In-app purchases

6. Data Retention & Deletion

All game data is stored locally in your device's app storage. You can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app. No data is retained on external servers.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
